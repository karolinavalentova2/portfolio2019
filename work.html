The working process

"First drafts, consultations, implementation and many revisions."

After being approached by a client my focus is on understanding the customers needs. I begin my work with initial small drafts of possible ideas quickly refining each one until I get one that fits my requirements. By the time I have a good draft of a product, I immediately start designing a prototype followed by a quick draft of an interactive prototype to get a better picture of the users flow of my idea. Afterwards, I begin my final step into developing the product whilst still refining it to be of an acceptable quality and feel.
